Project description

Tomorrow Tech is a French mobile phone reconditioning startup. It proposes to arrange the shopping centers and other places of affluence of terminals. These kiosks are lockers equipped with cameras and sensors that can scan phones. This helps identify and optimize trips, as well as repairs, by grouping phone types. The goal is to reduce repair and reconditioning costs, while increasing quality and service lead times.

I was in charge of the IA part of the project. I had to implement an image recognition algorithm to identify phones. Having set up a scrapping system to retrieve as much information on phones from open-source databases, we used PyTorch to train a model of convolutive neural network. Finally, we used MangoDb to store phone information.

As the project is private, further technical details cannot be shared.
